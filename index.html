My PHR – Patient Management System

A hybrid between EMR and PHR to connect patients and healthcare providers.

My PHR is my thesis project: a patient management system that combines features of an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) and a Personal Health Record (PHR). The goal is to make health information more accessible while keeping it secure and well-structured for both patients and clinics.

  • Role: Designer & Developer
  • Focus: Healthcare, Data Management
  • Tech: Web technologies & secure data handling

Lava Vault – Jira Extension

A password vault integrated into Jira for secure team collaboration.

Lava Vault is a Jira extension developed with a university team, with the goal of launching it on the Atlassian Marketplace. It helps organizations securely store, share, and manage passwords within Jira. It also supports strong password generation, inspired by the idea of using lava lamps as a randomness source for enhanced security.

  • Role: Team Member / Developer
  • Focus: Security, Collaboration
  • Platform: Jira / Atlassian Ecosystem

Student Accommodation Platform

Helping students find housing easily without extra fees.

As part of the stUDinnovate Innovation Program at the University of Debrecen, our team worked on a platform that connects students with partner agencies to find accommodation more easily. The focus is on transparency, support, and ensuring students don’t have to pay extra service fees to us.

  • Role: Team Member
  • Program: stUDinnovate
  • Focus: Student Services, UX
